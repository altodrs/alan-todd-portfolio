Overview

The Glasgow Times is the city's daily newspaper with a deeply local identity. The design challenge was dual: creating slick, conversion-driven subscription marketing assets — while also spearheading the visual design of The Wee Red Book, one of Scottish football's most beloved annual publications.

My work focused heavily on capturing local community spirit and driving reader participation through energetic, locally-resonant visual design.

Case Study
The Challenge

Refresh the visual appeal of legacy campaigns like the Wee Red Book to appeal to younger audiences without alienating lifelong readers.

The Strategy

Combined nostalgic community-focused design elements with punchy, actionable call-to-actions optimised for quick reader conversions.

The Execution

Designed cross-platform competition landing pages, eye-catching retail point-of-sale displays, print ads, and comprehensive promotional layouts for the annual launch.

The Results

Boosted seasonal subscription retention and generated high levels of public engagement for both print campaigns and digital contests.
