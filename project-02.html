Conceptualised and executed dynamic design assets for a diverse portfolio of high-profile sporting, marketing, and event campaigns. I delivered visually compelling narratives that aligned with The Herald's prestigious editorial standards — bridging the gap between fast-paced sports coverage and premium corporate event branding.

Overview

The Herald is Scotland's oldest daily newspaper and one of the UK's most respected quality titles. Working within the brand's editorial framework, I conceptualised and executed campaign design across a wide spectrum — from the prestige of the Herald Scottish Business HQ Awards to the electric energy of the UEFA Euro 2024 campaign.

This work required a highly agile approach: the ability to shift visual register rapidly, maintaining brand credibility whether designing for a black-tie business ceremony or a high-energy live sports event.

Case Study
The Challenge

Maintain a prestigious national identity while creating high-energy, modern graphics for diverse marketing events and fast-moving sporting campaigns.

The Strategy

Utilised bold typography, dramatic imagery layout, and a refined colour palette to capture the excitement of live events and sports coverage.

The Execution

Produced large-scale event signage, digital promotional assets, print supplements, interactive web graphics, and editorial marketing collateral.

The Results

Enhanced audience engagement at live events and elevated the visual profile of The Herald's sports and marketing initiatives across Scotland.
