Years of Experience

I'm a graphic designer with over 21 years of experience keeping design standards high at Newsquest Scotland / The Herald and Times, The Scottish Farmer, and more. My approach to design is simple: I take a rough creative brief and turn it into a striking final asset that connects with people.

For over two decades, I've evolved from print and traditional layout design into video, motion graphics, and animation. No fuss, honest communication, and getting the job done right the first time.

How I Work

MY PROCESS

01
Listen & Research

Every project starts with understanding — the audience, the history, the competitive space. Good marks come from knowing what's already been said.

02
Concept

Wide exploration before narrowing down. I work through dozens of directions before committing — because the second idea is rarely the right one.

03
Craft & Refine

Details are where design earns its keep. Kerning, weight, texture, spacing — the gap between good and great lives in these decisions.

04
Deliver & Document

A brand handed off without a system is a brand that degrades. I document every decision so teams can extend the work with confidence.
