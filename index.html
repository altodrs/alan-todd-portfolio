About

ALAN
TODD
Designer

21+
Years of Experience

I'm a graphic designer with over 21 years of experience keeping design standards high at Newsquest Scotland / The Herald and Times. My approach to design is simple: I take a rough creative brief, steady the ship, and turn it into a striking final asset that connects with people.

The design landscape moves fast. Over two decades, I've evolved from print and traditional layout design into video, motion graphics, and animation. I thrive on fast deadlines, require no hand-holding, and act as a reliable pair of hands in the studio. No fuss, honest communication, and getting the job done right the first time.

How I Work

THE
PROCESS

01
Listen & Research

Every project starts with understanding — the audience, the history, the competitive space. Good marks come from knowing what's already been said.

02
Concept

Wide exploration before narrowing down. I work through dozens of directions before committing — because the second idea is rarely the right one.

03
Craft & Refine

Details are where design earns its keep. Kerning, weight, texture, spacing — the gap between good and great lives in these decisions.

04
Deliver & Document

A brand handed off without a system is a brand that degrades. I document every decision so teams can extend the work with confidence.
