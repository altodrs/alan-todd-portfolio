Overview

The National is Scotland's only daily newspaper to advocate for Scottish independence — a title with a passionate, politically-engaged readership and a distinctive visual identity built around its bold editorial voice.

My work spanned subscription campaign design, major news event graphics, and the creation of election data visualisations for the Scottish Parliament elections — translating complex political data into clear, engaging visual stories for a digital-first audience.

Case Study
The Challenge

Synthesise dense political statistics and fast-moving election data into instantly understandable graphics under tight newsroom deadlines.

The Strategy

Developed a highly structured, colour-coded design system to break down voting data, candidate information, and regional maps.

The Execution

Created real-time social media infographics, map visualisations, print feature layouts, and digital headers for rolling election night coverage.

The Results

Provided readers with a highly accessible visual guide to the elections, which increased digital dwell time and social media shares significantly.
