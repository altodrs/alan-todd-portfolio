Led the print and digital design strategy for high-profile subscription campaigns and commercial collaborations. Working with Scotland's premier agricultural publication, I translated complex client briefs into high-impact marketing assets that successfully engaged the rural community and strengthened brand loyalty across both physical and digital touchpoints.

Overview

The Scottish Farmer is Scotland's longest-running and most widely-read farming publication. As lead designer, I was responsible for the full visual output — from weekly print layouts to multi-channel digital campaigns and commercial partnership assets.

A flagship challenge was the Ardbeg × Scottish Farmer collaboration — a premium whisky brand requiring an editorial aesthetic that felt native to the publication while elevating the advertiser's premium positioning.

The Challenge

Create cohesive campaign designs that resonate with a traditional agricultural audience while modernising digital subscription acquisition.

The Results

Delivered a unified brand message that drove measurable increases in subscription sign-ups and established stronger commercial partnerships.
