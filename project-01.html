Project Overview

Led the print and digital design for a high-profile subscription collaboration between Scotland's premier agricultural publication, The Scottish Farmer, and the iconic Islay single malt brand, Ardbeg.

The brief called for a visual language that could sit comfortably in both the earthy, trusted world of agricultural print media and the premium, atmospheric brand environment Ardbeg occupies. The result bridges heritage and craft — heavy textures, controlled editorial layouts, and a colour palette drawn directly from the peat and smoke of Islay.
